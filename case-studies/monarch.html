The client

A 14-person advisory serving high-net-worth clients.

Monarch Solutions is a U.S.-based insurance advisory serving high-net-worth individuals. Their team of 14 manages a variety of complex workflows across the business — portfolio reviews, client onboarding, compliance, and proposal generation for use on client calls.

Among those workflows, the generation of proposals stood out as both critical and deeply inefficient.

The problem

An hour of copy-paste and formatting on every proposal.

Before automation, building a single proposal could take up to an hour. Team members jumped between multiple documents, copy-pasting details, dealing with format conversions, and fighting the inconsistencies that inevitably crept into the final deliverables.

Every proposal required double-checking for errors and reformatting tables before it could be sent. The process was time-consuming, error-prone, and a drag on both growth and client experience — the kind of manual bottleneck that's easy to tolerate for a while but impossible to scale past.

The build

CRM-native proposal generation.

We deployed a custom proposal generation system embedded directly within Monarch's CRM. An AI component analyzes uploaded insurance data, organizes it into clean structured tables, and places everything into a polished, brand-consistent PDF.

The entire flow — from raw client data to a client-ready proposal — lives inside the tool Monarch already uses every day, with no jumping between platforms and no format conversions.

The new workflow

A few clicks inside the CRM.

A team member uploads insurance data into the CRM. The AI layer reads and structures it. A branded PDF is generated and attached to the client record automatically. The whole sequence completes in a few clicks.

The impact

A single well-optimized process, compounding everywhere.

Proposal creation time was cut by 50%, manual errors dropped to near zero, and every document now ships in a consistent format aligned with Monarch's portfolio and brand.

What used to consume valuable team hours — and, more importantly, the team's best attention — is now a streamlined workflow that frees the staff to focus on higher-value work. A single well-optimized process unlocked efficiency and consistency across every client-facing deliverable the firm produces.

The relationship with Monarch is ongoing.
